
	Stonehenge. It is a place with enormous stones. It is considered to be built by a magician called Merlin. It is forbidden to come close to the stones or walk between them. But the view itself is amazing

	Loch-Ness Lake. The name of the Loch-Ness monster is known all around. The story began when a couple saw an animal coming out of the lake. The journalist who was told this story spent a lot of time longing to see the monster coming out.
	
	Durham Cathedral. It is a wonderful place to see for those who are fond of Harry Potter. It is the world-famous castle known as Hogwarts for the most of the world.
